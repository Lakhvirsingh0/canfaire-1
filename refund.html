1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for the following scenarios:

  • Subscription or premium services purchased directly through the Site, if applicable.
  • Technical issues resulting in non-delivery of a paid service.
  • Accidental duplicate charges.

Please note that refunds do not apply to third-party bookings, transactions, or agreements made with hotels, booking platforms, or other service providers.

2. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Contact us at info@canfaire.com with the subject line “Refund Request.”

We aim to review and respond to all refund requests within 5-7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not granted in the following cases:

  • Dissatisfaction with a hotel or service booked through a third party.
  • Change of mind after completing a subscription or premium service purchase.
  • Incomplete refund requests lacking necessary information or proof of purchase.

4. Third-Party Bookings

Canfaire does not handle hotel bookings or reservations directly. Refunds for bookings made through third-party providers must be handled by the respective platform or hotel according to their policies. We recommend reviewing their terms and conditions prior to completing a booking.

5. Processing Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method used during the purchase. Depending on your payment provider, it may take 5-10 business days for the funds to reflect in your account.

6. Changes to This Refunds Policy

Canfaire reserves the right to update or modify this Refunds Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. Continued use of our Site after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
